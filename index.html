Our Story

From a family kitchen in Istanbul to the heart of Sydney.

Eat Istanbul was built on a simple idea: that the food you grab on a busy workday should taste like it was made by family. Owner Ekrem Yildiz brought the recipes, spices and time-honoured techniques of Istanbul's street kitchens to Sydney — and refused to cut a corner.

Every kebab is marinated and charcoal-grilled to order. Every gözleme is rolled by hand. The result is honest Turkish street food — bold, generous and full of flavour — now served fresh across four Sydney locations.
